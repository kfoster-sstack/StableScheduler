Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

StableScheduler ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the StableScheduler website and scheduling tool (the "Service"). StableScheduler is operated as part of the Schneider Saddlery Equine Network.

1. Information We Collect

Email Address

When you sign up to access the StableScheduler tool, we collect your email address. This is the only personally identifiable information we actively collect.

Scheduling Data

All scheduling data you create within the tool — including lesson schedules, staff shifts, and daily tasks — is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (localStorage). This data is not transmitted to our servers. It remains entirely on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our servers may automatically collect standard technical information when you visit, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

This information is used for basic analytics and to ensure the Service functions properly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To send you product updates and announcements related to StableScheduler (via email)
  • To communicate information about the Schneider Saddlery Equine Network that may be relevant to you
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the Service
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

3. Data Storage and Security

Your scheduling data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your lesson schedules, staff shifts, or task lists. If you clear your browser's local storage or use a different browser/device, this data will not be available.

Your email address is stored securely on our systems and is protected by industry-standard security measures.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your email address within the Schneider Saddlery organization for purposes consistent with this Privacy Policy. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Cookies

StableScheduler uses localStorage (not cookies) for application data. Our website may use minimal cookies for basic analytics and functionality. These do not track you across other websites.

6. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to other websites operated by Schneider Saddlery or third parties. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you (your email address)
  • Delete your scheduling data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage
  • Unsubscribe from email communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in our emails
  • Request deletion of your email address from our systems by contacting us

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Schneider Saddlery network at sstack.com.
