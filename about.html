About StableScheduler

Part of the Schneider Saddlery Equine Network

StableScheduler was built out of a simple frustration that every barn manager knows: the endless text chains, whiteboard scribbles, and sticky notes that pass for a "schedule" at most equine facilities. We believe barn operations deserve better tools — tools that are simple, free, and built by people who actually understand horses.

What We Do

StableScheduler is a free, browser-based scheduling tool designed specifically for equine facilities. It handles the three core scheduling needs of any barn:

  • Lesson Scheduling — A weekly calendar with color-coded lessons by instructor, conflict detection for double-booked horses or arenas, and support for private, semi-private, and group lessons.
  • Staff Shift Management — Assign staff to feeding, mucking, turnout, lesson support, maintenance, and office duties. Pre-built shift templates make weekly scheduling fast. Automatic weekly hour tracking keeps labor organized.
  • Daily Task Board — A checklist-style board pre-loaded with common barn tasks like AM Feed, Turnout, Muck Stalls, and Evening Check. Add custom tasks, mark them complete with timestamps, and reset daily.

How It Works

StableScheduler runs entirely in your browser. All scheduling data is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage. There are no accounts to create, no software to install, and no monthly fees. Simply enter your email to access the tool and start scheduling.

Privacy First

Your scheduling data never leaves your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your barn's operational data on our servers. The only information we collect is your email address, which is used solely to grant access to the tool and to send occasional product updates. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
